Product Requirement Document (PRD)
Project Name
StudyVault – Notes and Resource Marketplace
Problem Statement
Students often struggle to find quality notes, previous year papers, assignments, and study materials for their courses. Resources are scattered across multiple platforms, making them difficult to access and share.
Solution
StudyVault is a centralized platform where students can upload, browse, download, and share academic resources. The platform allows students to find study materials based on subject, semester, and course.
Target Users
College Students
Faculty Members
Student Clubs and Study Groups
Objectives
Provide a single platform for academic resources.
Enable easy upload and download of study materials.
Improve collaboration among students.
Reduce time spent searching for notes.

Market Research – StudyVault

1. Problem Identification

Many students face difficulties in finding quality study materials such as:
>Lecture notes
>Previous year question papers
>Assignments
>Lab manuals
>PPTs and reference books

Most resources are shared through WhatsApp, Telegram, Google Drive, or personal contacts, making them difficult to organize and access.
2. Existing Solutions

Platform with limitations 

>Google Drive
No subject-wise organization

>WhatsApp Groups
Files get lost in chats

>Telegram Channels
Difficult to search specific notes

>College Portals
Limited resources available

3. Target Audience

>Undergraduate students
>Postgraduate students
>Faculty members
>Study groups

4. Survey Findings

>65% students struggle to find notes before exams.

>35% students use WhatsApp groups for study material.

>70% students prefer a centralized platform for resources.

>90% students want subject-wise categorization.

5. Market Opportunity

The increasing use of digital learning platforms creates demand for a centralized academic resource-sharing system. StudyVault can become a one-stop solution for students.
