Product Requirement Document (PRD)
Project Name
StudyVault – Notes and Resource Marketplace
Problem Statement
Students often struggle to find quality notes, previous year papers, assignments, and study materials for their courses. Resources are scattered across multiple platforms, making them difficult to access and share.
Solution
StudyVault is a centralized platform where students can upload, browse, download, and share academic resources. The platform allows students to find study materials based on subject, semester, and course.
Target Users
College Students
Faculty Members
Student Clubs and Study Groups
Objectives
Provide a single platform for academic resources.
Enable easy upload and download of study materials.
Improve collaboration among students.
Reduce time spent searching for notes.

4. Resource Download

User Flow

User uploads study material.
Material is categorized and stored.
Other users search for resources.
Users view, rate, and download resources.

StudyVault simplifies access to academic resources by providing a centralized and user-friendly platform for sharing and discovering study materials.
